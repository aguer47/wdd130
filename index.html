South Sudan

South Sudan is a landlocked country located in East-Central Africa, bordered by Sudan to the north, Ethiopia to the east, Kenya and Uganda to the south, and the Democratic Republic of Congo and the Central African Republic to the west. The country is rich in cultural diversity, with over 60 ethnic groups, each with its own traditions, music, and dances. Popular cultural activities include traditional wrestling, storytelling, and vibrant dances often performed during weddings and community celebrations, reflecting the deep pride South Sudanese people have in their heritage.
